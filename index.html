About

DesignBi is an Architectural Service and Graphical Design company, based in Northamptonshire, offering services nationwide.

The primary purpose of our company is to provide low cost services to the local community whilst offering a high quality design concept that meets the requirements of clientele and provides an innovative successful design suitable to client requirements.

We have completed both residential and commercial projects with expertise ranging from project management to planning and building regulations. The projects are known to vary in complexity which makes it crucial for us to offer a bespoke service to each individual client.

We have developed a great understanding with the local authority which has allowed us to build a reputable name both within the industry and local community. In addition, we have an in house team of approved contractors which gives us the advantage of completing the project from start to finish.
